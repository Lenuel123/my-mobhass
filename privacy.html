Personal data we collect


We collect the information you provide when you use the application, including when you sign up for an account. Our systems automatically process information or data you provide as a client. According to Republic ACT NO. 10173, also known as the data privacy act, a law that seeks to protect all forms of information, be it private, personal, or sensitive. The Information that clients put on MOBHASS mobile app, website logins, and sign-ups must be protected. And rest assured, MOBHASS is always protecting any kind of data that the user provides for the application.(https://www.privacy.gov.ph/data-privacy-act/)

HOW WE USE PERSONAL DATA

We collect information about how you use our application, such as the features you use; the actions you take; and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities. For example, you log in when you're using and have last used the website, MOBHASS also collects information about how you use features like camera, or you turn off the lights, the power outlet, and such. Rest assured that you, as a client, and the data you provide for MOBHASS are well protected and will not be publicly revealed, as we, the MOBHASS team, will get your consent.

MOBHASS ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

With a MOBHASS account, you can sign into MOBHASS products; Personal data associated with your MOBHASS account includes credentials, name and contact data, payment data, device and usage data, your contacts, and information about your activities.

When you create your own MOBHASS account tied to your personal email address, we refer to that account as a personal MOBHASS account.

If you sign into a service offered by a third party with your MOBHASS account, you will share with that third party the account data required by that service.

You can’t delete nor manage your information on the application, but you may contact the administrator for further assistance. When you request the admin to delete or manage your account, admins will delete or edit the information you provided on the application, including the contact, email address, and the home address you put.
