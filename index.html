Here are some of the products available with MOBHASS

DOOR (RFID TECHNOLOGY)

We are removing the traditional way of accessing your door using an RFID (Radio-frequency identification) door locking system that offers more security and convenience to your home. MOBHASS featured a fixed RFID reader, which is commonly mounted on walls and is attached to a given location. To unlock and open the door, the household member simply taps his or her registered key card into the RFID reader.

Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) is the non-contact wireless transmission of data using radiofrequency waves. In an RFID door lock access control system, each household member's credential (typically a keycard or fob with an RFID chip) contains unique identifying information called a tag.

INTERNET OF THINGS (IoT) DEVICES or MICRO-CONTROLLER DEVICES

The micro-controller device allows household members to control electronic devices via an app using WiFi connectivity, and we have included a manual button to turn on and off the switches. Turning on or off your devices like light bulbs (auto volts) and fans (220 - 240 volts), within your app is an effortless and quick way of regulating their state.

The window lock system can keep your home more protected by providing the homeowner access to control and manage the status of a window by closing and opening through an app. A servomotor is linked to the nodeMCU, which is capable of connecting items and transferring data via the Wi-Fi protocol.

POWER OUTLET

The power outlet allows members of the household to control and track these devices remotely using their smartphones. This may be used to plug in other electronic devices, such as appliances that we use on a daily basis. This type of outlet, such as Parallel, parallel with grounding, universal, two-way outlet, and two-way with grounding, are suits different types of houses.

GAS or SMOKE SENSOR

These gas or smoke detectors and alarms are critical life-saving devices that must remain operational in case of a fire. When the alarms sense or become triggered due to the density of smoke and gasses, it will send SMS notifications to the homeowner and the nearest authority.

CCTV (IP CAM)

MOBHASS used a smart CCTV (closed-circuit television) camera called IP Cam. The important benefit of this camera is that it can stream within a significant distance of your home. It continuously records or obtains footage of the scope of events by the camera, which is stored on an SD Card.

Aside from security surveillance, MOBHASS integrated it with facial recognition and it will send a notification to the application, which helps identify the intruder or unauthorized personnel. Household members’ may review recent recordings and captured faces, including the name, in the data acquired and preserved in storage.

About

MOBHASS is an IoT Based Home Security System where users may remotely monitor and control virtually the IoT devices in their houses, which can help them avoid home-related incidents. Smart houses technologies significantly collect and analyze real-time data to improve home energy efficiency and sustainability as well as residents' security, comfort, and safety.

MOBHASS is a group of students founded concurrently in 2021 for their Capstones 1 and 2. The team considers that we are in an era where almost everything can be controlled with a single click or touch. The group aims to design an Internet of Things-based home security system that can be managed and monitored using our smartphones.
