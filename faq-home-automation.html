- Mobhass or Mobile-based Home Automation Security System is an application where you can control installed IOT Devices, also it helps your home to be more safe and secure. Also, Mobhass has RFID which is installed on your door, therefore, we have a smoke sensor that automatically notifies customers using SMS, and last but not least we have CCTV cameras to see if some unknown person enters your house.

- MOBHASS will be featuring four (4) IoT devices that you can include in the package that you want. With MOBHASS, you can remotely control your light, fan, power outlet, and window. Inside the application, there will be a toggle switch where you can change the state of your devices even if you are not at home. You have the option to add how many and what devices you are gonna include in your package.

- Yes, you can upgrade the use of an existing IoT device with smart home technologies. by plugging the devices into an IoT outlet device, which allows you to turn them on and off using a mobile application. The concept behind these IoT outlet devices is that you can stop the flow of electric current in the outlet using a mobile application, enabling you to turn on and off a device.
